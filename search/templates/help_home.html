FDGAdb is a powerful tool that could help researchers to explore RNAs functional mechanisms in FDs and seek the FDs-associated genome data sets. The current version of FDGAdb documents 9999 associations between 999 RNAs and 99 FDs in 9 species, manually collected from 99999 published literatures (Figure 1). Meanwhile, FDGAdb collected information of 8888 genome date sets of FDs.
